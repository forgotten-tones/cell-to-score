# Semester 1: Berklee Requirements

## Official Course: Private Composition Lesson Level 1

*Private Composition Lesson Level 1* is an introduction to formal composition studies. The instructor will assess your skill level and evaluate your history of composition training and skills to plan a course of study that supports your learning trajectory and goals. Level 1 focuses on composing for a **solo instrument (excluding piano)**. Emphasis is on developing individual creativity in contemporary musical styles.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this semester, you will be able to:

- Analyze contemporary composition styles and techniques through the study of representative scores
- Develop your compositional techniques and apply them toward writing a piece for a solo instrument
- Consider musical form and musical coherence in your compositions
- Apply contemporary techniques, skills, and strategies to the craft of composition for a solo instrument
- Notate the score according to professional notation standards
- Reflect on your compositional process and articulate artistic choices and intentions
- **Compose a piece for a solo instrument (excluding piano)**

## Requirements

- Notation software (Sibelius, Dorico, MuseScore, etc.) or handwritten notation
- Weekly submission of work-in-progress
- Final polished score in PDF format
